# Two-Day Hiking Plan for the Grand Canyon (April 17-18, 2025)

### Weather Advisory for April 17, 2025

**Conditions**: Mostly sunny with occasional rain and snow showers.
- **High Temperature**: 52°F (11°C)
- **Low Temperature**: Around 30°F (-1°C)
- **Precipitation**: 20-30% chance of rain and snow showers.
- **Wind**: Northeast wind speeds ranging from 8 to 14 mph, with gusts as high as 21 mph.
- **Humidity**: 28%
- **Dewpoint**: 19°F (-7°C)
- **Barometer**: 29.95 in (1014.22 mb)

### Two-Day Hiking Plan for April 17-18, 2025

**Trail Selection**: South Kaibab Trail to Skeleton Point and overnight camping at Cedar Ridge

#### Day 1 (April 17, 2025):
1. **Start Time**: Early morning, around 7:00 AM to maximize daylight.
2. **Trailhead**: Begin at the South Kaibab Trailhead.
3. **Timeline**: 
   - **7:00 AM – 8:30 AM**: Hike to Ooh Aah Point (0.9 miles)
   - **8:30 AM – 10:00 AM**: Continue to Cedar Ridge (1.5 miles further)
   - **10:00 AM – 11:30 AM**: Reach Skeleton Point (1.4 miles further)
   - **11:30 AM – 12:00 PM**: Rest and enjoy the view at Skeleton Point.
   - **12:00 PM – 1:30 PM**: Begin return hike to Cedar Ridge.
   - **1:30 PM – 2:30 PM**: Set up camp at Cedar Ridge.
   - **2:30 PM – 5:00 PM**: Explore the surrounding area and enjoy the scenic views.
   - **5:00 PM – 6:00 PM**: Prepare and have dinner.
   - **6:00 PM – 8:00 PM**: Relax and enjoy the sunset.
   - **Overnight**: Camping at Cedar Ridge

**Total Distance**: Approximately 3.8 miles.

#### Day 2 (April 18, 2025):
1. **Start Time**: Early morning, around 7:00 AM.
2. **Timeline**:
   - **7:00 AM – 8:00 AM**: Breakfast and pack up camp.
   - **8:00 AM – 9:30 AM**: Begin return hike to Ooh Aah Point.
   - **9:30 AM – 11:00 AM**: Hike back to South Kaibab Trailhead.
   - **11:00 AM**: Arrival at South Kaibab Trailhead / End of hike.

**Total Distance**: Approximately 3.8 miles.

**Hiking Difficulty**: Moderate to strenuous due to elevation change.
**Estimated Duration**: Approximately 8-9 hours for the full hike over two days including breaks and overnight stay.

### Safety Tips:
- Check for updated weather conditions before starting your hike.
- Inform someone about your hiking plan and expected return time.
- Stay on marked trails and avoid venturing off-trail.
- Be prepared for sudden weather changes, especially given the chance of rain and snow showers.
- Descend at your own pace and avoid exerting yourself too much on the ascent.
